What is BarterUP

Do you have items around the house that you don't use anymore and they just take up space? BarterUP was created to give users a platform to get rid of their unwanted junk in exchange for something they wanted or needed. You do not need to pay any money and you can get something useful in exchange for items you were going to throw out anyway.

Just post any unwanted items in your profile and wait for someone to offer you something in exchange for your item. You will have even better luck if you tell users what items your looking to trade for.
